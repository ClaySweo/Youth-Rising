Klamath Falls, Oregon
The Youth Rising Drop-In located in Klamath Falls, OR was the first location developed by Tasha Locks, CEO, who is pictured above. This drop-in is a place for young adults to learn and practice skills needed for job interviews and college applications, while socializing with peers with fun activiites like foosball, pool, and learning guitar.
